About the metrics
Wk-RMS — 1-second RMS of vertical acceleration (simplified, no full Wk biquad; uses high-pass at 0.4 Hz). ISO 2631-1 comfort: <0.315 comfortable, >2.0 extremely uncomfortable.

VDV — 4th-power vibration dose value, cumulative. HGCZ daily limit 8.5–17.

Lateral g / Roll — vehicle-frame lateral acceleration and roll angle from gravity vector.

Calibration — done at trip start while stationary, using gravity to find vertical and GPS course to find longitudinal.

Sample rate: ~60 Hz (browser-limited). Native Swift app would hit 800 Hz.
Learning (Bayesian recommender)
Observations from your trips train the recommendation engine. After 10 observations on a given road type, the app uses your data instead of the manufacturer table.
